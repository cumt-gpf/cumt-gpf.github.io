Biography

Pengfei Gao is a fifth-year Ph.D. student at ShanghaiTech University under supervision of Prof. Fu Song. His research interests are broadly in the area of program analysis, verification, and testing. He is mainly working on developing new techniques to formally verify side-channel resistance of masked implementations and design secure and efficient masked implementations.
